My Skills

User Research

Getting to know my user through quantitative and qualitative methods. Gaining a deep understanding of their experiences and needs, in order to identify pain points. These insights serve as the foundation to produce solutions that will have a positive impact.

User Empathy

Synthesising user feedback and thinking from the perspective of my potential user. Employing a human-centered design approach in order to understand my users’ emotions, motivations and needs. Good listening skills to gather insights from users.

Qualitative Interviews

reating an environment in which users feel comfortable to share their insights. Using broad, open questions to ensure freedom of speech, will in turn enable me to have an in depth understanding of my user and their experiences.
